CryptoPulse Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 2, 2026

CryptoPulse ("we", "our", or "us") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the CryptoPulse mobile application. By using the application, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account, we may collect:

  • Email address
  • Authentication information through Firebase Authentication

The application may also collect:

  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) device token for push notifications
  • Basic device information required for application functionality and security
  • User preferences such as saved portfolio and watchlist

CryptoPulse does not collect or store:

  • Bank account details
  • Credit or debit card information
  • Government-issued identification
  • Cryptocurrency wallet private keys
  • Recovery phrases (Seed Phrases)

2. Market Data

Cryptocurrency prices, market capitalization, trading volume, and related information are obtained from trusted third-party market data providers.

Currency exchange rates are obtained from trusted exchange-rate providers.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Authenticate your identity
  • Store your watchlist and portfolio
  • Send push notifications you have enabled
  • Improve application performance and reliability
  • Maintain application security
  • Provide customer support

4. Third-Party Services

CryptoPulse uses trusted third-party services including:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM)
  • Cryptocurrency market data providers
  • Currency exchange-rate providers
  • Google AdMob (for displaying advertisements)

These services may process limited information necessary to provide their functionality according to their own privacy policies.

5. Advertising

CryptoPulse displays advertisements using Google AdMob. AdMob may collect certain device identifiers and advertising-related information in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy to deliver and measure advertisements.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

Information is shared only with trusted service providers required for operating the application, such as Firebase and Google AdMob.

7. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures, encrypted network communications (HTTPS), and secure authentication to protect your information. However, no electronic transmission or storage system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

8. Data Retention

Your account information is retained while your account remains active.
